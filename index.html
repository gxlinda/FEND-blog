I have my own Human!

02.10.2019

I'm soooooooo happy! I waited a whole month at the shelter for this moment! My caretakers told me, that someone will come, and my life will change, but I still can't believe it really happened! A Mum-Human and a Daughter-Human came just because of me, and they adopted me at once! Can you believe that?

Saved from death

01.09.2019

I felt so bad... It was cold, I was hungry, and nobody took care of me. Humans did not want to help me, as I had no fur due to a bacterial infection. But all of a sudden that Lady-Human came to me, put a towel around me, and took me with her. I was brought to an animal rescue shelter, where everyone was so nice to me. But the best part was, that I got food to eat, and I was not hungry anymore.
