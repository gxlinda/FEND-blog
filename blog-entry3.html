What the woof is Corona?

20.03.2020

Strange... All humans stayed home last week. Even the Dad-Human, who usually leaves to work early, and arrives only late afternoon. Woof, what happened to them? Actually I love this situation, as I get more hugs and cuddlings, and more MEAT too. They mention something about corona, but I do not understand what that is.

If corona means that my loved ones are around me, and we take care of each other, and play much much much more together, than I simply love that corona! We even make much longer walks than before in the near forest. As a wise dog ones said:

Taking time together with your human is Heaven!


The only thing I miss is the dog-school. There was only one session, and after that it was forbidden to go, due to that corona-thing. I miss my dog-friends, we could play so well together...
